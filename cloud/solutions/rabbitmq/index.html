RabbitMQ on Google Cloud Platform

RabbitMQ is a fast, dependable and easy to use open source messaging broker that supports a wide range of message-oriented middleware use cases

Try it now or Contact sales

Click to Deploy RabbitMQ cluster handles over 1 million messages per second

RabbitMQ is an open source messaging system that serves a variety of use cases. Through Click to Deploy feature, Google Cloud Platform makes it easy to instantiate a dedicated RabbitMQ cluster in a matter of few minutes. Our RabbitMQ test clusters were able to sustain a throughput of over 1 million messages published and consumed per second (a sustained combined ingress/egress of over two million messages per second), validating it as a great fit for high throughput and low latency scenarios.

Benefits of RabbitMQ on Google Cloud Platform:

  • Robustness and Flexibility

    RabbitMQ supports messaging over a variety of messaging protocols. There are RabbitMQ clients for almost any language you can think of.

  • High Throughput

    Google Cloud Platform automatically deploys a suitably clustered RabbitMQ configuration capable of sustaining higher loads and throughputs than a single broker.

  • High Availability

    Easily take advantage of RabbitMQ’s mirrored queue HA feature on Google Cloud Platform.
